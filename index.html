Why a timer you feel?

Audible timers pull everyone around you out of their own focus. A wrist tap reaches only one person — you. That makes Quietick at home in shared offices, libraries, lectures, and quiet meditation.

Because the cues are physical, you never have to check the screen mid-session. Your attention stays on the work, the workout, or the breath.

  • Stay focused in shared and silent spaces
  • No glance, no notification noise, no interruptions for others
  • Reliable cues even with the screen off
