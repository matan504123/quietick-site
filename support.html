Frequently asked questions

Why no sound?

Quietick uses Apple Watch haptics — small wrist vibrations — so your timer is only felt by you. It's designed for open offices, meditations, lectures, and any setting where audible timers would be disruptive. The goal is to keep you on schedule without pulling others into your rhythm.

Why does the timer stop after about an hour?

Apple Watch limits background runtime sessions to roughly one hour. This is an operating system constraint, not a Quietick limitation. After a session ends, you can simply tap Start again from the Quietick app on your Watch to begin a new one.

My haptics feel weak.

Open the Settings app on your Apple Watch, then go to Sound & Haptics and set Haptic Strength to Prominent. This is a system-wide setting that makes all haptic feedback — including Quietick's wrist taps — noticeably stronger.
